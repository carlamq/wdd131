High Vibes Path | Site Plan

Site Purpose

Shows general information about meditation, its benefits, and how to get started.

Scenarios

  • What is and what is the benefit of meditation?
  • How can I start to meditate?

Color Schema

  • Background: Soft color with high brightness and contrast #F5F2EB
  • Text: White for headings, black for body text.
  • Header, footer and menus: Dark violet #180C42, soft violet for hover effects. #DABFFF
  • Buttons: Blue, with dark violet for hover #2582f5.
  • Accent: Soft violet #DABFFF.

Fonts

  • Headings: Italiana, sans-serif.
  • Body text: Nunito, sans-serif.
